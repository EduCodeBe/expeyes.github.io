Op-amp Circuits

With the built-in Waveform generator, Oscilloscope and the +/- 6V DC supply, we can study Op-amp circuits upto 100kHz range. The amplitude of WG can be set to 3 volts, 1 volt or 80 mV. For testing amplifier circuits, set the amplitude to 80 mV. Input in monitored by A1 and the output is connected to A2. Several examples are given below
