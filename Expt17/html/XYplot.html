Study of Series RC circuit

A sinusoidal voltage is applied to a series RC circuit and the voltages across R and C are plotted. The resulting trace becomes a circle when R = Zc. The circle may not appear as a circle on the screen, need to look for the frequency where Xmax = Ymax.
