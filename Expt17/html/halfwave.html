Half wave rectifier

WG generates a sine wave. It is monitored by oscilloscope channel A1. Signal after the diode is monitored by A2. The waveform is observed. It will be a bit noisy without the load resistor, connecting a 1k resistor gives a clean rectified waveform. The voltage drop across the diode is clearly visible. Connect different values of capacitors to view the filtering effect.
